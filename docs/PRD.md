# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Masjid Syukur Website

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

Last Updated: June 2026

---

# 1. Product Overview

Masjid Syukur Website is the official digital platform of Masjid Syukur.

The platform serves as the primary source of information about the mosque, its activities, programs, management, announcements, and future community services.

The website is designed to be simple, trustworthy, accessible, and maintainable while providing a foundation for future expansion into donations, event registration, educational content, and community engagement.

---

# 2. Product Vision

To become the central digital hub for Masjid Syukur that connects the mosque with its congregation and the wider community through transparent information, meaningful content, and accessible services.

---

# 3. Product Goals

## Primary Goals

* Provide accurate and up-to-date information about Masjid Syukur.
* Increase visibility of mosque programs and activities.
* Improve public trust through transparency and documentation.
* Create a sustainable digital presence for the mosque.
* Enable non-technical administrators to manage content independently.

## Secondary Goals

* Document mosque activities and achievements.
* Increase community engagement.
* Prepare infrastructure for future donation systems.
* Support future educational content publishing.

---

# 4. Success Criteria

The project is considered successful when:

## Phase 1

* Visitors can easily find information about the mosque.
* Visitors can view available programs and activities.
* Visitors can contact the mosque through available channels.
* Administrators can update content without developer assistance.

## Phase 2

* Administrators regularly publish articles and activity documentation.
* Visitors can browse activity galleries and announcements.

## Phase 3

* Donation campaigns can be published and managed digitally.
* Visitors can contribute through supported donation methods.
* Financial transparency information can be published.

---

# 5. Target Users

## Public Visitors

People who want to:

* Learn about Masjid Syukur.
* Find mosque location and contact information.
* Explore mosque programs.
* Follow announcements and activities.

---

## Congregation (Jamaah)

People who want to:

* Stay informed about mosque activities.
* Participate in programs and events.
* Access educational content.
* Support mosque initiatives.

---

## Donors

People who want to:

* Learn about mosque programs.
* Understand the impact of donations.
* Support mosque activities financially.

---

## Administrators

Mosque management members who want to:

* Manage website content.
* Publish announcements.
* Update program information.
* Maintain digital communication.

Administrators should not require technical knowledge to perform daily content management.

# 6. Product Scope

Purpose:

Provide a concise overview of Masjid Syukur and guide visitors to important information.

Content:

* Hero Section
* About Masjid
* Featured Programs
* Call To Action
* Location Information
* Contact Information

---

### Profile

Purpose:

Introduce Masjid Syukur and its identity.

Content:

* History
* Vision
* Mission
* Organizational Structure
* Facilities

---

Purpose:

Showcase mosque programs and activities.

Examples:

* Religious Studies (Kajian)
* Social Programs
* Youth Activities

Allow visitors to reach the mosque easily.

Content:

* Address
* Maps

# 7. Non-Goals

The following are not priorities during early phases:

* Social networking features
* Community discussion forums
* Real-time chat systems
* Complex membership systems
* Native mobile applications
* Multi-tenant architecture

These may be evaluated in future phases if justified by actual needs.

---

# 8. Design Principles

The website must be:

* Simple
* Professional
* Trustworthy
* Accessible
* Mobile-first
* Fast-loading
* SEO-friendly
* Easy to navigate

Content clarity is prioritized over visual complexity.

---

# 9. Content Principles

Content should:

* Be accurate and factual.
* Reflect Islamic values.
* Be respectful and inclusive.
* Avoid unnecessary complexity.
* Prioritize usefulness for visitors.

The website should continuously demonstrate that Masjid Syukur is active, transparent, and beneficial to the community.

---

# 10. Technical Principles

The platform should:

* Prioritize maintainability.
* Prioritize simplicity.
* Minimize operational costs.
* Follow framework conventions.
* Avoid unnecessary dependencies.
* Be easy for future contributors to understand.

Technology choices should support long-term sustainability rather than short-term trends.

---

# 11. Future Vision

The long-term vision is to evolve from a company profile website into a comprehensive digital platform that supports:

* Information sharing
* Educational content
* Community engagement
* Donations
* Event management
* Digital services for the congregation

while maintaining simplicity, transparency, and sustainability.
