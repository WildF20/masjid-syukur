# Content Strategy

# Masjid Syukur Website

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

Last Updated: June 2026

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the content strategy for the Masjid Syukur platform.

The goal is to ensure the website remains:

* Active
* Useful
* Trustworthy
* Relevant

over the long term.

Content should demonstrate that Masjid Syukur is a living and active institution serving the community.

---

# 2. Content Philosophy

The website is not merely a digital brochure.

It should function as:

* Information Center
* Activity Documentation Platform
* Educational Resource
* Community Communication Channel

Content should continuously reflect the real activities and impact of the mosque.

---

# 3. Content Objectives

Primary Objectives:

* Inform visitors
* Build trust
* Document activities
* Promote programs
* Encourage participation

Secondary Objectives:

* Improve discoverability through search engines
* Support future fundraising efforts
* Preserve institutional history

---

# 4. Content Pillars

All content should belong to one of the following pillars.

---

## Pillar 1: Mosque Identity

Purpose:

Introduce Masjid Syukur.

Examples:

* History
* Vision and Mission
* Management Structure
* Facilities
* Mosque Profile

Update Frequency:

Rare

Expected Changes:

Only when organizational information changes.

---

## Pillar 2: Programs & Activities

Purpose:

Show ongoing activities.

Examples:

* Weekly Kajian
* Youth Activities
* Educational Programs
* Social Programs
* Ramadan Activities

Update Frequency:

Monthly

Importance:

Very High

---

## Pillar 3: Educational Content

Purpose:

Provide beneficial Islamic knowledge.

Examples:

* Islamic Articles
* Reflections
* Study Notes
* Friday Sermon Highlights

Update Frequency:

Weekly or Biweekly

Importance:

High

---

## Pillar 4: Activity Documentation

Purpose:

Show evidence of mosque activity.

Examples:

* Event Reports
* Community Service Activities
* Program Recaps
* Photo Documentation

Update Frequency:

After each major activity

Importance:

Very High

---

## Pillar 5: Announcements

Purpose:

Communicate important information.

Examples:

* Schedule Changes
* Public Notices
* Event Invitations
* Ramadan Information

Update Frequency:

As Needed

Importance:

High

---

# 5. Homepage Content Strategy

The homepage should always communicate three things:

1. The mosque exists.
2. The mosque is active.
3. The mosque is beneficial.

---

## Homepage Sections

### Hero Section

Purpose:

Provide a clear introduction.

Recommended Content:

* Mosque Name
* Short Tagline
* Main Call To Action

---

### About Section

Purpose:

Introduce the mosque briefly.

Maximum Length:

150-250 words

---

### Featured Programs

Purpose:

Highlight important programs.

Recommended Count:

3-6 items

---

### Recent Activities

Purpose:

Show recent activity.

Recommended Count:

3-6 items

---

### Latest Articles

Purpose:

Promote educational content.

Recommended Count:

3-5 items

---

### Contact Information

Purpose:

Provide quick access to communication channels.

---

# 6. Program Content Strategy

Each program should include:

Required:

* Title
* Description
* Category

Recommended:

* Schedule
* Location
* Contact Person
* Cover Image

Optional:

* Registration Link
* Related Documents

Programs should focus on usefulness rather than marketing language.

# 8. Gallery Content Strategy

Gallery should focus on storytelling.

Avoid:

* Random photo collections
* Unorganized uploads

Preferred Structure:

Event

↓

Album

↓

Photos

Examples:

* Ramadan 1448 H
* Community Service Program
* Mosque Renovation

---

# 9. Announcement Strategy

Announcements should be short and actionable.

Examples:

Good:

* Kajian Schedule Update
* Eid Prayer Information
* Registration Opening

Avoid:

* Long-form articles disguised as announcements

---

# 10. Donation Content Strategy

Future Phase

Donation campaigns should clearly explain:

* Purpose
* Target Amount
* Expected Impact
* Progress

Transparency is more important than persuasion.

---

# 11. Content Governance

Every published content should have:

* Author
* Publish Date
* Status

Statuses:

* Draft
* Published
* Archived

---

# 12. Content Lifecycle

Content should follow a lifecycle.

Draft

↓

Review

↓

Publish

↓

Archive

Content should never be deleted unless necessary.

Historical records are valuable.

---

# 13. Writing Guidelines

Content should be:

* Clear
* Respectful
* Informative
* Inclusive
* Easy to understand

Avoid:

* Excessive formal language
* Clickbait titles
* Misleading information

---

# 14. SEO Guidelines

Content should prioritize human readers.

SEO should support content quality, not dominate it.

Every article should include:

* Meaningful title
* Summary
* Relevant keywords naturally

Avoid keyword stuffing.

---

# 15. Success Indicators

The content strategy is successful when:

* Website is updated consistently.
* Visitors can understand mosque activities.
* Programs are well documented.
* Community trust increases.
* Administrators can maintain content easily.

---

# 16. AI Agent Instructions

When generating content-related features:

1. Prioritize content management simplicity.
2. Support content lifecycle workflows.
3. Support future content growth.
4. Preserve historical records.
5. Avoid designs that require technical knowledge from administrators.

Always optimize for long-term content sustainability.
